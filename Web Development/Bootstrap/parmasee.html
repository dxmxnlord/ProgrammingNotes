About Our Project



Overview

Searching

Shopping


Pharma-See is an web-based project that acts as an online pharmacy. It's purpose is to serve as an easy way for anybody to obtain information regarding a particular drug. As a result both consumers and doctors can achieve an insight on any particuar drug which can be of tremendous value. It incorporates a searching and shopping service.

The searching feature allows anyone to look at a vast record collection of drugs on the market and look into the details of each and every drug including cost, side-effects, symptoms, dosages, diseases, other related drugs, and manufacturer.

The shopping freature allows anybody with proper authority or licence to purchase drugs from an online store for retail/personal use and even locate nearby shops that sell a required drug. This saves a lot of time if the drug is obscure.
